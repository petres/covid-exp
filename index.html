Covid19 Cases Projection for Austria

Extrapolates the covid case numbers by using a simple exponential approximation of the recent data.

Code: https://github.com/petres/covid-exp
Data Source: BMSGPK, Ã–sterreichisches COVID-19 Open Data Informationsportal (https://www.data.gv.at/covid-19)

Because the case data used are reported by date of diagnoses, the last one/two days are not included in the projection because the case count will be revised upward substantially in future updates. Side note: Therefore, the reported AGES incidence number are always biased downwards.

The interactive graph allows you to adjust the initial date of the approximation period.
